shakemap.grind.gmpe2shakemap module¶

Routines to convert GMPE amps to/from ShakeMap values GMPE amps are in ln(g), in an IMC of their choosing ShakeMap amps are %g and max horizontal

shakemap.grind.gmpe2shakemap.ampGmpeToShakeMap(gmpe_amps, gmpe, imt)[source]¶

Convert ground motion amplitudes from a GMPE to match the and intensity measure component (IMC) used by Shakemap and convert from natural log to linear units.

Parameters:
  • gmpe_amps – Numpy array of ground motion amplitudes from a GMPE.
  • gmpe – An Openquake GMPE.
  • imt – An Openquake IMT.
Returns:

Numpy array of converted ground motion amplitudes.
