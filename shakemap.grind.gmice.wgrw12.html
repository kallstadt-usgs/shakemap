shakemap.grind.gmice.wgrw12¶

class shakemap.grind.gmice.wgrw12.WGRW12[source]¶

Bases: object

Implements the ground motion intensity conversion equations (GMICE) of Worden et al. (2012).

References:
Worden, C. B., Gerstenberger, M. C., Rhoades, D. A., & Wald, D. J. (2012). Probabilistic relationships between ground‐motion parameters and modified Mercalli intensity in California. Bulletin of the Seismological Society of America, 102(1), 204-221.

getGMfromMI(mmi, imt, dists=None, mag=None)[source]¶

Function to tcompute instrumental intensity from macroseismic intensity. Supported PSA are for 0.3, 1.0, and 3.0 sec periods.

Parameters:
  • mmi – Macroseismic intensity.
  • imt – OpenQuake IMT of the requested instrumental intensities (must be one of PGA, PGV, or SA). [link] <http://docs.openquake.org/oq-hazardlib/master/imt.html>
  • dists – Numpy array of distances (km).
  • mag – Magnitude (float).
Returns:

Ground motion amplitude; linear units; %g for PGA and PSA, cm/s for PGV.

Function to compute macroseismic intensity from an instrumental intensity. Supported PSA are for 0.3, 1.0, and 3.0 sec periods.

Parameters:

  • imt – OpenQuake IMT of the input amps (must be one of PGA, PGV, or SA). [link] <http://docs.openquake.org/oq-hazardlib/master/imt.html>
  • dists – Numpy array of distances (km).
  • mag – Magnitude (float).
Returns:

Macroseismic intensity.
